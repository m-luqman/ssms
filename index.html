To learn minimum number of new topics per day
Goal 2
To revise topics with low retention
Goal 3
To learn partially covered topics
Goal 4
To self-test the topics of high retention

Strategic planning
Procedure: Survey the following components of a topic:
  • Diagrams
  • Diagram captions
  • Subheadings
  • Tables
  • Table captions
  • Words in bold/italics
  • Words suffixed by "-" or ":"
  • Sentences with question mark
Output: List of key artifacts of the topic.
Input: Output of survey
Procedure: Convert what was surveyed into questions. Center your questions around the following:
  • Purpose
  • Definitions
  • Description
  • Importance
  • Levels of abstractions
  • Costs
  • Trade-offs
  • Assumptions
  • Comparisons and contrasts
  • Examples
Output: List of questions.
Input: Output of question
Procedure:
  1. Read the topic seeking to answer the questions formulated earlier.
  2. Extract answers from the text by bracketing, underlining, highlighting etc.
Output: List of answers.
Output: List of answers.
Assesment criteria
Self assesment must be done by self-explaning the output of strategic planning,
  1. While looking at the output. (If unable to self explain, read the topic only so much so as to be able to coherently correlate the items in the output list)
  2. Without looking at the output.
